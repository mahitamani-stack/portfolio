FAQs

Why Showit?

When it comes to DIY-ing your website, there really is no better platform than Showit because it:

• Gives you total creative freedom in customising and creating your website.

• Requires absolutely no coding skills, whatsoever.

• Integrates with Wordpress, the best (and most comprehensive) blogging platform.

• Has eCommerce capabilities (through Shopfiy Lite).

• Is easy to integrate with third party apps like Flodesk, Dubsado, etc.

With our completely custom projects, Webflow is our preferred platform. But, as a creative who will be implementing, customising, and maintaining your website on your own, Showit is a great option and resource for you. Showit gives our templates longevity–because you can easily keep your site up-to-date. It’s instantly ready to grow alongside your business.

Will these templates work with other platforms?

Nope, our templates were built in Showit, for Showit! They are not compatible with other website building platforms like Webflow, Squarespace, or Wix.

How much should I expect to pay for my new website all-in?

The total amount will vary based on a few factors. But, here is an overview of what you can expect to spend on your website:

• SHOWIT SUBSCRIPTION: you can find monthly/yearly pricing outlined HERE. If you have (or want) a blog on your website, you will need to opt for tier 2 or tier 3 pricing.

• DOMAIN: ~$15 per year. We recommend hosting your domain through Google, that way you can easily get an email address with your domain name.

• TEMPLATE: a one-time investment, easy peasy.

• COPY & PHOTOGRAPHY: These are two elements of your website that are important to consider. They can be an investment (we have recommendations), or you can DIY them. Either way, you won’t want to overlook them before you launch your new site. All of our templates include strategic copy prompts and usable stock images if you feel like going the DIY route.

Where do I even start after I purchase my template?

Don’t worry! After checkout, you’ll be instantly redirected to our help site including your share key that allows you to install your template on your Showit account, and all of our video tutorials. We’ll be with you every step of the way through the surprisingly simple process of getting your site ready for lift-off. You can take your template from purchased to edited to live within a week.

Can I keep the images in my template?

Absolutely! All images featured in our templates are royalty free stock images.

Yes, but one purchase = one project (meaning one website for one business). If you're looking to regularly use the same template as part of your process to deliver customised websites to multiple clients... let's chat about a designer licensing deal. Drop us a line. We don't bite (pun intended).
