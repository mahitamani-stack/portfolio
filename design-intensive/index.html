And how do we do it?

Prep work

Apply for a day that works with your schedule, keeping in mind that you will need to be by your computer on that day to give feedback.

Complete an in-depth brand discovery questionnaire (including visual inspiration) 4+ days ahead of your strategy call.

Meet for the scheduled strategy call 4+ days before your selected intensive day to establish the creative direction, clearly define objectives, and approve the order of priorities.

Day of

We'll spend the full eight hours designing, revising, and perfecting your brand’s visual design.

Throughout the day, we’ll send across your assets in order of priority and request your timely feedback. This is required to ensure that we complete as much as possible during your intensive day. Any revisions will be implemented in real time.

At the end of the day, you will receive a link with your final files and style guide. We’ll walk you through your new brand so you can confidently implement your new identity moving forward. Easy-peasy!

Branding

How is a Design Intensive different from a "typical" design project?

With full branding and website projects, we spend weeks going back and forth with research, presentations, meetings, and revisions, and place a heavy focus on brand strategy. In an intensive structure, we are able to pare all of that back into a day’s worth of work (plus prep) by cutting out the back and forth and focusing on what's essential. That means less time spent on presentations and meetings, more time spent designing.

How do I know if a Design Intensive is for me?

Do you want professional branding or a website that's tailored to your goals? Are you on a modest budget? Do you expect things in your business to change quickly, and/or want to be able to launch ASAP? Can you make decisions fast? Do you know your target market and business well? If the answer to all of this is yes, then the Intensive was made for you!

It's important to note that we rely on your participation so that the result will be strategic as much as visually appealing.

If you need help deciding whether a full project or a Design Intensive is the better fit for you and your business, feel free to reach out to hi@bitemark.studio.

Who will be working on my designs?

Your Intensive will be a 1:1 collaboration with our founder and lead designer Martha. While we can arrange to have your team members join the initial strategy call (should you have any) so we can establish the vision all together, we ask that you appoint one decision maker to provide feedback on the design day. This will give you the best results.

Is there a chance we won’t complete my design in the 8 hours?

A Design Intensive is an investment in our time rather than a set of deliverables. That means the outcome can vary depending on your priorities, the complexity of your design, and the speediness of your feedback. We can’t promise any amount of deliverables but we wouldn't be offering this service if we weren't proud of the (consistently amazing) results. The usual feedback is shock at how much we can actually get done. Hell, we were told that we should get an award for the sheer ability to create unique brands in essentially a day! Check out examples of past projects in our portfolio.

Do you use templates?

Never for branding, but of course during a web focused Design Intensive since it's essentially a customisation of one of our Showit templates.

What if I need branding and a website?

Simple, you can book two Intensives! Since there are a handful of things that we need on your end before we can start the project (copywriting, photography, etc.) please email us to discuss the timeline.

What if we’re not in the same time zone?

Zero problem! While we're based in Poland (CET time zone), the majority of our clients are US-based. In such circumstances where the time zones are too different, we'll simply adjust the schedule: The Intensive will be split in half so that it’s two half days instead of one full day. So even if your time zone is wildly different from ours, don’t be discouraged. We've got a plan!

Do you offer payment plans?

Absolutely, because we want to make design as accessible as possible for everyone. You'll be able to make your choice during the inquiry.

Do you have examples? Reviews? Somewhere to send questions to?

Yes, yes, and yes! Send any requests to hi@bitemark.studio.

What type of branding can I expect to get?

Our usual: strategic, different, personality-packed. Despite the short timeframe, we don't skimp on quality. All branding is bespoke to you and never from a template.

The exact outcome depends on a couple of factors such as the complexity of your design, your desires, and the speediness of your feedback on the day. With efficient communication, you'll get a minimum of logo, colour palette, and type suite guaranteed. In the past, we've always had time for extras such as brand elements, Instagram templates, and stock photo sourcing. We've found that most brands need more than fonts and colours to create graphics with ease, that's why our focus is on giving you a comprehensive identity system.

To see specific examples of branding created during an Intensive, check out our portfolio page or email us to see more results. Should you have something particular in mind, send us an email to discuss if we can pull it off during an Intensive or if a full branding project might be a better fit.

How does my involvement differ from a typical branding project, and what does that mean for the final outcome?

While both project structures ultimately give you a visual identity that strategically conveys your message, the outcome during an Intensive more heavily depends on your input. That is: your answers regarding your business & target audience in your initial questionnaire, and your feedback on the design day. It's a matter of letting ideas marinate over the span of minutes, not days or even weeks.

Don’t worry though, we don't put you in the only director's chair. Throughout the entire project—but especially in the early phase of establishing your creative direction—we'll share our ideas and push you outside of your comfort zone to elevate your vision beyond what you think possible. An Intensive is a tight 1:1 collaboration.

Who pays for fonts?

We do, up to $200. If you're now wondering how much fonts are: many are free, most cost between $30 and $50. With brands typically needing between 2 and 4 fonts, $200 is a healthy budget. Still, before purchasing any licenses on your behalf, we consult with you.
