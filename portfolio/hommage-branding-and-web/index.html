Domino's Sri Lanka — Menu Photography & Burger Films

Context

Domino's Sri Lanka needed their full menu shot — 80 products across pizzas, sides, and a rice range. Their whole team flew in to Delhi for the stills shoot. We had a local Sri Lankan chef on set to help us navigate the menu. He was great.

Problem

We'd never worked with the Sri Lankan menu before. Different toppings, different ranges, different everything. Figuring out how to style and present each category correctly — without being familiar with the food — was the main challenge.

Solution

Nine shoot days, a lot of prep, and a very organised pre-production deck. The Sri Lankan team was collaborative throughout. The burger films — a separate brief, done over video call — came together the same way.

The Concept

80 products means 80 chances to get it wrong. The prep work was everything — shoot order, tabletop choices, propping per category, how each pizza style should be presented differently from the next. Once that was sorted, the shoot itself could just flow.
