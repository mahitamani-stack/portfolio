Context

By the time Burger King India launched Korean Spicy Fest, Korean food was everywhere — grocery aisles, social media, everywhere. They were the first QSR to do it properly, nationwide. A La Carte Pictures made the product films.

Problem

The brief needed a visual world that felt Korean without being costume-y about it. This was a cultural moment for Burger King India — their biggest new menu launch in years. The films had to match that.

Solution

The background treatment reference I suggested answered the brief — Korean, high energy, but all about the food. Dark red-black surfaces, dramatic lighting made the food pop and shine.
